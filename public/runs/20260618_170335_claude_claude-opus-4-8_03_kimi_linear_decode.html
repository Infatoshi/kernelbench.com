Core requirement — fused int4 dequant-GEMV (Triton): streams the int4 weights once and fuses unpack + per-group asymmetric dequant directly into the GEMV using the group identity y = Σ_g s_g·(Σ x·w − z_g·Σx), with split-K + atomics and autotuned configs.
