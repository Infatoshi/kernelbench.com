The two ideas the problem highlights, plus aggressive fusion:

  1.   1. MLA "absorb" — query/output are projected through kv_b in the 512-d latent space (kv_b pre-dequantized once at load), so the growing KV cache is read compressed instead of expanding to [L, H·256] every step. This makes latency nearly flat across context length.
  1.
