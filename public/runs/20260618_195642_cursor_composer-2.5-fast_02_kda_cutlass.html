The op is the chunk-parallel KDA forward from the FLA library: q and k of shape (B, T, H, K) in bf16, v of shape (B, T, H, V) in bf16, g of shape (B, T, H, K) in fp32 (per-channel log-decay with in-chunk cumsum already applied), beta of shape (B, T, H) in bf16, scale a python float, chunk_size 64, no initial state, no final state.
